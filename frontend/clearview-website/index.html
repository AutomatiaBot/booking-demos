When to call urgently

If you have any of these symptoms, call us right away:

  • Sudden vision loss or major sudden vision changes
  • Eye injury/trauma or chemical exposure
  • Severe eye pain or severe redness
  • Flashes, sudden floaters, or a curtain/shadow over vision

Frequently asked questions

If you don’t see your question here, call us and we’ll help you pick the right next step.

Do I need an exam to get contact lenses?

Yes—contact lenses require a current contact lens exam and fitting. Please call (555) 203-7811 and we’ll guide you.

I have dry, burning, or gritty eyes—what should I do?

Dry eye symptoms vary by person. We recommend an evaluation so we can understand what’s driving your symptoms and discuss in-office options.

Do you see children?

Yes. We offer pediatric eye exams and age-appropriate testing. Call us and we’ll help determine the best visit type.

Is this an emergency?

If you have sudden vision changes, injury/chemical exposure, severe pain, or flashes/floaters, call us immediately. If severe and you can’t reach us, seek urgent care/ER.

How much does an eye exam cost?

Costs depend on the exam type and your needs. For a personalized estimate, please call (555) 203-7811 or email hello@clearviewvision.example.
