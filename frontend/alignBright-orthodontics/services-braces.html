Braces

Home / Services / Braces

Effective, Reliable Results

Traditional braces remain one of the most effective orthodontic treatments. Modern braces are smaller, more comfortable, and more efficient than ever before.

Request a Consultation

Types We Offer

Metal Braces

The most common type—durable, effective, and now smaller and more comfortable. Great for patients of all ages and all types of orthodontic issues.

Ceramic Braces

Tooth-colored brackets that blend with your smile. An esthetic option for patients who want less visible braces while maintaining effectiveness.

Ideal For

  • Complex alignment cases
  • Significant bite corrections
  • Patients who prefer fixed treatment
  • Children, teens, and adults

What to Expect

Braces are bonded to your teeth and connected with wires that apply gentle pressure. You'll visit our office regularly (typically every 4-8 weeks) for adjustments. Treatment length varies by case.

Caring for Braces

Brush after every meal
Avoid hard, sticky, and chewy foods
Use a floss threader or water flosser
Wear a mouthguard for sports
