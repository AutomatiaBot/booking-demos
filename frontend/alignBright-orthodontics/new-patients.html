New Patients

Home / New Patients

Welcome to AlignBright Orthodontics! We're excited to meet you and help you achieve a beautiful, healthy smile.

Your First Visit

Your initial consultation is a chance for us to meet, discuss your goals, and evaluate your orthodontic needs. We'll take digital scans or images of your teeth and explain your treatment options.

Please arrive a few minutes early to complete any paperwork. Bring your insurance card if you have orthodontic coverage.

What to Expect

  • Welcome and brief medical history review
  • Digital scans or X-rays of your teeth
  • Discussion of your goals and concerns
  • Explanation of treatment options
  • Estimated timeline and cost overview

Schedule Your Consultation

To schedule your consultation, please call our office at (562) 555-0117. Our team will find a convenient time and answer any questions you have.

Insurance & Payment

We accept most major dental insurance plans with orthodontic coverage. Our team will help verify your benefits and explain your coverage.

We also offer flexible payment plans to make treatment affordable. Ask us about financing options during your consultation.

Patient Forms
