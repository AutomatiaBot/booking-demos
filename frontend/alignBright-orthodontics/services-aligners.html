Clear Aligners

Home / Services / Clear Aligners

Straighten Your Teeth Discreetly

Clear aligners are custom-made, removable trays that gradually shift your teeth into alignment. They're virtually invisible, comfortable, and fit seamlessly into your lifestyle.

Request a Consultation

Ideal For

  • Adults seeking a discreet option
  • Teens with compliance commitment
  • Mild to moderate alignment issues
  • Patients who want removable treatment

How Aligners Work

You'll receive a series of custom aligners, each worn for about 1-2 weeks. Each set makes small adjustments to tooth position, gradually moving teeth into alignment.

Aligners should be worn 20-22 hours per day, removed only for eating, drinking (except water), and brushing. Regular check-ins ensure you're progressing as planned.

Benefits

Nearly invisible appearance
Removable for eating and cleaning
No food restrictions
Comfortable smooth plastic
Fewer office visits
Easy to maintain oral hygiene

Aligners for Teens

We offer aligner programs designed for teens, with compliance indicators to help track wear time. Our team provides coaching and support to help teens stay on track with their treatment.
