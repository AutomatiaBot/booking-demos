About AlignBright

Home / About

Modern Orthodontics with a Personal Touch

AlignBright Orthodontics was founded on a simple belief: everyone deserves access to modern, comfortable orthodontic care delivered with genuine compassion.

Located in Lakewood Hills, CA, we serve patients of all ages—from children getting early evaluations to adults seeking discreet treatment options. Our team combines advanced technology with personalized attention to create beautiful, healthy smiles.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional orthodontic care in a welcoming environment, using modern techniques to help every patient achieve their best smile.

Our Values

Patient-Centered Care

Every treatment plan is customized to your unique needs and goals.

Clear Communication

We explain every step so you can make informed decisions.

Modern Excellence

We stay current with the latest techniques and technology.

Our Approach

We believe orthodontic treatment should fit your life—not the other way around. That's why we offer flexible scheduling, multiple treatment options, and clear guidance on costs and timelines.

Whether you're considering clear aligners for their convenience or traditional braces for their precision, we'll help you find the right path to your goals.

Part of the Community

We're proud to serve Lakewood Hills and surrounding communities. Our practice is built on relationships—many of our patients are referrals from happy families who trust us with their smiles.
