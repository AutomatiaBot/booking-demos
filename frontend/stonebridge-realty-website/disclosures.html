Wire fraud warning

Cyber criminals sometimes impersonate parties in a real estate transaction and send convincing messages that "change" payment details. For your safety:

  • Do not trust wiring instructions delivered through email, chat, or text.
  • Independently verify any payment details by calling a trusted, verified phone number.
  • If you receive a "rush" request or last-minute change, treat it as suspicious until verified.

This is general safety information for demo use only.
