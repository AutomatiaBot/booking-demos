Common conditions we help with

Not sure where you fit? Call and we'll route you to the right next step.

  • Low back pain, sciatica-like symptoms
  • Neck pain, posture-related strain, headaches linked to tension
  • Shoulder pain, rotator cuff irritation, impingement-like symptoms
  • Hip pain, knee pain, patellofemoral pain
  • Sprains/strains, tendonitis, plantar heel pain
  • Post-surgical rehab (with protocol)
  • Balance concerns and fall risk
Important: We don't diagnose or provide medical advice online. If you have severe symptoms, call 911 or go to the nearest ER.

Not sure if it's urgent?

If you have any of the following, seek urgent medical care:

  • Chest pain, trouble breathing, fainting
  • New loss of bowel/bladder control
  • Severe trauma or suspected fracture/dislocation
  • Sudden severe weakness or facial droop

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you get started. If you don't see your question, call us.

Do I need a referral to start physical therapy?

It depends on your insurance plan and state regulations. In Florida, you can often see a physical therapist directly for evaluation. Call us at (555) 418-6620 and we'll confirm what you need based on your specific situation.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We work with most major insurance plans. Please call (555) 418-6620 with your insurance details, and we'll verify your benefits and explain any out-of-pocket costs before your first visit.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Wear comfortable clothing that allows movement. Bring your ID, insurance card, referral (if you have one), imaging reports, and any post-op protocols from your surgeon. You can also email these ahead of time.

How much does physical therapy cost?

Costs vary based on your insurance coverage and treatment needs. We'll provide a personalized estimate after verifying your benefits. Call (555) 418-6620 or email intake@restoremotionpt.example for details.

How long are the therapy sessions?

Initial evaluations typically last 45-60 minutes. Follow-up sessions are usually 30-45 minutes. We focus on quality one-on-one time—you won't be shuffled between multiple patients.

How many sessions will I need?

This varies by condition and goals. After your evaluation, we'll give you an estimated plan of care. Some issues resolve in 4-6 visits; others may need longer. We adjust as you progress.

Do you offer telehealth or virtual sessions?

Currently, we focus on in-person care for hands-on assessment and treatment. However, we can discuss options for follow-up guidance if travel is difficult.

What conditions do you treat most often?

We specialize in orthopedic conditions—back/neck pain, joint injuries, post-surgical recovery, sports injuries, and balance issues. If you're unsure whether we can help, call and describe your situation.

Can I come in without being referred by a doctor?

In Florida, direct access allows you to see a physical therapist without a referral for evaluation and treatment. Some insurance plans may still require a referral for coverage—we'll help you figure this out.

What happens if my condition doesn't improve?

We track progress at each visit. If you're not improving as expected, we reassess the approach, consider additional evaluation, or refer you to another provider if appropriate. Your progress matters to us.

Do you work with post-surgical patients?

Yes, we frequently help patients recover after orthopedic surgeries like ACL reconstruction, rotator cuff repair, hip/knee replacement, and spinal procedures. We follow your surgeon's protocol.

What makes RestoreMotion different from other PT clinics?

We emphasize 1:1 focused sessions, clear communication, and measurable goals. You'll always know what we're doing and why. We don't overbook or rush—your recovery gets our full attention.
