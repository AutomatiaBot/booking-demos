About Harborlight

Our philosophy

We believe therapy should feel safe, practical, and respectful. Our clinicians focus on clear goals, measurable progress, and compassionate support—without judgment.

  • Warm, structured sessions
  • Clear next steps after each visit
  • Collaborative planning
  • Trauma-informed approach

Mission: Make it easier to start and stay in care by reducing friction and providing a calm, professional experience.

Values: Safety • Respect • Clarity • Accountability • Inclusion

Who we serve

Adults, couples, and teens (13+) seeking support for stress, anxiety, mood changes, relationship challenges, and life transitions.

If you’re unsure what type of support you need, call us and we’ll help you decide.
