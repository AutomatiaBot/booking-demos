Services

Home / Services

We provide therapy services that are structured, practical, and tailored to your goals. We can help you decide what fits during a brief intake.

Individual Therapy

Adults

Support for anxiety, stress, depression support, burnout, self-esteem, coping skills, and life transitions.

Formats: In-person or virtual

Couples Therapy

Partners

Communication skills, conflict patterns, intimacy repair, rebuilding trust, and premarital counseling.

Formats: In-person or virtual

Teen Counseling (13+)

Adolescents

Support for school stress, anxiety, mood support, confidence, and family transitions. Parent/guardian involvement as appropriate.

Formats: In-person or limited virtual

Approaches we may use

  • Skills-based therapy (coping, communication, emotion regulation)
  • Trauma-informed care (stabilization and support)
  • Goal setting and progress review
  • Mindfulness and stress reduction tools

Note: We don’t diagnose or provide crisis counseling through the website.

Common questions we can answer

  • What’s the difference between individual and couples therapy?
  • Do you offer virtual sessions?
  • How does teen counseling work with parents/guardians?
  • What should I expect in the first session?
