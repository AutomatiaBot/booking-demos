# Harborlight Counseling & Wellness — Brand Guide (Fictional Demo)

## Brand essence
**Positioning:** Evidence-based therapy and counseling delivered with warmth, clarity, and respect.  
**Personality:** Calm, grounded, hopeful, professional.  
**Voice:** Short sentences. Simple words. Non-judgmental. Consent-forward.  
**Do:** Offer clear next steps; normalize help-seeking; use inclusive language.  
**Don’t:** Promise outcomes; use clinical jargon excessively; provide crisis counseling online.

## Logo
- **Primary:** Lighthouse + soft halo (symbolizes steady guidance).
- **Wordmark:** “Harborlight” in Fraunces, “Counseling & Wellness” in Inter.

## Typography
- **Headings:** Fraunces (variable serif)
- **Body/UI:** Inter (variable sans)
