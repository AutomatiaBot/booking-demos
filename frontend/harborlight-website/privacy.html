Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Overview

Harborlight Counseling & Wellness ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address when you submit forms or contact us
  • Message Content: Information you provide in contact forms or inquiries
  • Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, and time spent on our site
  • Cookies: We use cookies to improve your browsing experience

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and requests
  • To coordinate scheduling and appointment reminders
  • To improve our website and services
  • To communicate important updates about our practice
  • To comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third parties who assist in operating our website or conducting our business
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Professional Services: As necessary for providing clinical services, with your consent

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@harborlightcounseling.example.

Emergency Use Notice

Do not use this website for emergencies. If you are in immediate danger, call 911. In the U.S., call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.
