Skills

Grounding exercises

Try 5-4-3-2-1: name five things you see, four you feel, three you hear, two you smell, one you taste.

Stress

Breathing reset

Inhale for 4, hold for 2, exhale for 6. Repeat for 2–3 minutes to reduce physiological arousal.

Couples

Repair attempts

Practice short “repair” phrases: “Can we restart?” “I hear you.” “I’m getting overwhelmed—pause?”
