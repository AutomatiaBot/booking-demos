What to expect

A simple, respectful process designed to reduce friction and help you start.

Step 1 — Quick intake

  • Call us at (555) 641-2289 or email contact@harborlightcounseling.example.
  • We’ll ask a few questions about what you’re looking for and whether you prefer virtual or in-person.
  • You’ll receive basic paperwork and policies.

Step 2 — First session

  • We’ll focus on your goals, what’s been hard lately, and what support is most useful.
  • You’ll leave with clear next steps (no pressure, no judgment).
  • We do not provide crisis counseling via this website.
