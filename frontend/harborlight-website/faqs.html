Frequently Asked Questions

Home / FAQs
Do you offer virtual sessions?

Yes. We offer secure virtual sessions as well as in-person visits in Brookhaven. Call us to confirm what fits your needs.

What happens in the first session?

Your first session focuses on what you’re dealing with, your goals, and what support might be helpful. We’ll also review basic policies and next steps.

Do you work with teens?

Yes—teen counseling is available for ages 13+. A parent/guardian will be involved in the intake process as appropriate.

Can you help if I’m in crisis right now?

This website can’t provide crisis support. If you’re in immediate danger, call 911. In the U.S., call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (24/7).

Do you provide diagnoses or medication?

We don’t diagnose or prescribe medication through the website. For clinical questions, please call the office to discuss next steps.

How do I schedule?

Please call (555) 641-2289 to schedule. You can also email contact@harborlightcounseling.example for non-urgent requests.

Still have questions?

Send a message and we’ll point you to the right next step.
