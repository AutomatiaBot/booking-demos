Our intake process

We keep it structured and simple—so you know what happens next.

  1. Quick intake: matter type, urgency, and key dates.
  2. Conflicts check: we confirm we can speak with you about the matter.
  3. Consultation: we review documents and discuss options.
  4. Representation: begins only after a signed engagement agreement.

When to call immediately

Some situations should not wait for an email response.

  • Safety risk: threats, violence, or immediate danger → call 911.
  • Urgent deadlines: hearing dates, court filings, or detentions → call (555) 508-4402.
  • Injury emergencies: severe symptoms → seek medical care first.
