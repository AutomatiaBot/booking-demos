Practice Areas

Focused services with a consistent, step-by-step intake process.

Personal Injury

We help with intake and case evaluation for common injury claims, including auto accidents and premises injuries.

Common matters

  • Car, rideshare, and pedestrian accidents
  • Slip & fall and unsafe property conditions
  • Dog bites
  • Work-related injuries (intake and referral as appropriate)

What to prepare

  • Incident date, location, and a short summary
  • Photos (if available) and insurance claim info
  • Medical visit dates (no detailed medical history needed in chat)
  • Any upcoming deadlines you're aware of
Note: We cannot estimate case value or give fault analysis in chat. Please call (555) 508-4402 for a consultation.

Immigration

We provide process-focused guidance for common immigration pathways and help clients prepare for next steps.

Common matters

  • Family petitions
  • Work authorization (EAD)
  • Adjustment of status (green card pathways)
  • Naturalization / citizenship

What to prepare

  • Goal (petition, EAD, adjustment, citizenship)
  • Key dates and any notices (RFE, interview, biometrics)
  • General timeline (no sensitive ID numbers via chat)
  • Urgency: hearings, detentions, imminent deadlines
Important: Do not share passport numbers, SSNs, or A-numbers via chat. For urgent matters, call (555) 508-4402.

Family Law

We support clients with divorce, custody, and support matters with an emphasis on respectful communication and documentation.

Common matters

  • Divorce and separation
  • Custody and visitation
  • Child support and spousal support
  • Modifications to existing orders

What to prepare

  • County/state where the matter is filed (or will be filed)
  • Any existing orders and upcoming court dates
  • Whether children are involved
  • Any immediate safety concerns (call 911 if danger)
Safety: If there is an immediate danger or threats of violence, call 911.
