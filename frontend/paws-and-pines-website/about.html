About

Paws & Pines Veterinary Care is a fictional demo clinic built to showcase a modern veterinary website experience—clear services, strong calls-to-action, and safety-first guidance.

Brand tone

  • Calm: reduce anxiety with simple language and clear next steps.
  • Trustworthy: no diagnosis online; exam-first care and safe routing.
  • Efficient: one question at a time, fast scheduling handoff by phone.
Design system Primary color: Pine Green (#1B4332). Accent: Warm Amber (#D97706). Background: Cream (#F7F3E9).

What we treat

We focus on dogs and cats, with common visit types that are easy to demo:

  • Wellness exams and vaccines
  • Sick visits (stable, non-emergency)
  • Spay/neuter consult flow
  • Dental cleanings and follow-up
  • Microchipping

Accessibility & clarity

This demo uses clear navigation, readable typography, strong contrast, and obvious calls-to-action—helping more pet owners take the next step.
