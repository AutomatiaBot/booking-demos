About Us

Paws & Pines Veterinary Care provides compassionate, modern veterinary services for dogs and cats in Cedar Glen, CO—with clear communication, strong guidance, and a calm environment.

Our Story

Founded with the belief that veterinary care should be accessible, professional, and calming, Paws & Pines offers a welcoming space for pets and their families. Our team of experienced veterinarians brings expertise in wellness care, sick visits, procedures, and preventive medicine.

Whether you're bringing in a new puppy for their first vaccines or helping a senior pet stay comfortable, we're here with clear next steps and genuine care.

Our Philosophy

  • Calm: We reduce anxiety with simple language and clear next steps.
  • Trustworthy: No diagnosis online—your pet's exam comes first.
  • Efficient: One question at a time, fast scheduling by phone.
Our Promise We respect your time with straightforward communication and handle urgent cases with care.

What We Treat

We focus on dogs and cats, offering comprehensive care:

  • Wellness exams and vaccines
  • Sick visits (stable, non-emergency)
  • Spay/neuter procedures
  • Dental cleanings and follow-up
  • Microchipping
