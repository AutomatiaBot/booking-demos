Emergency signs

If you notice any of the below, seek urgent help:

  • Trouble breathing, collapse, seizure
  • Uncontrolled bleeding
  • Suspected toxin ingestion
  • Hit-by-car or major trauma
  • Cannot urinate or severe pain
