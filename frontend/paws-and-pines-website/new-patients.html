Emergency Guidance

New Patients

A simple first visit. We’ll collect your info, your pet’s basics, and the reason for the visit—then confirm next steps by phone.

What to bring

  • Any prior veterinary records (vaccines, labs, discharge notes)
  • Current medications and known allergies (if applicable)
  • Notes on symptoms (when it started, changes in appetite, behavior)
Tip If you have records ready, you can email them to frontdesk@pawsandpinesvet.example and call to confirm receipt.

Common first-visit questions

  • Is your pet a dog or cat, and about how old?
  • Is this wellness, a sick visit, a procedure, or dental care?
  • What symptoms are you seeing and when did they start?
  • Any vomiting/diarrhea—and how many times?
  • Any chance of toxin exposure or foreign object ingestion?
