Services

We keep it simple: understand the problem, build a plan, measure progress, and adjust along the way.

Orthopedic Rehab

Back, neck, shoulder, hip, knee, and ankle/foot care—strength, mobility, and movement retraining.

Post-Op Rehab

Guided recovery after procedures like ACL, rotator cuff, meniscus, and joint replacement—per your provider’s protocol.

Sports Injury Care

Return-to-sport progressions, running-related injuries, and overuse problems with clear milestones.

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques paired with exercise for mobility and symptom relief (we’ll explain what we’re doing and why).

Balance & Fall Prevention

Gait and balance training, confidence-building progressions, and fall-risk screening support.

Work Conditioning

Job-demand conditioning and safe return-to-work plans for physically demanding roles.

Common conditions we help with

Not sure where you fit? Call and we’ll route you to the right next step.

  • Low back pain, sciatica-like symptoms
  • Neck pain, posture-related strain, headaches linked to tension
  • Shoulder pain, rotator cuff irritation, impingement-like symptoms
  • Hip pain, knee pain, patellofemoral pain
  • Sprains/strains, tendonitis, plantar heel pain
  • Post-surgical rehab (with protocol)
  • Balance concerns and fall risk
Important: We don’t diagnose or provide medical advice online. If you have severe symptoms, call 911 or go to the nearest ER.

Not sure if it’s urgent?

If you have any of the following, seek urgent medical care:

  • Chest pain, trouble breathing, fainting
  • New loss of bowel/bladder control
  • Severe trauma or suspected fracture/dislocation
  • Sudden severe weakness or facial droop

How it works

A calm, structured process—so you always know what’s next.
