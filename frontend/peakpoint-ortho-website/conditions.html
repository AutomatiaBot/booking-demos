Conditions we treat

Below are common concerns we help evaluate and route. This list is not exhaustive.

Ask a question

Knee

  • Meniscus concerns
  • ACL/ligament injuries (evaluation & rehab routing)
  • Runner’s knee / patellofemoral pain
  • Arthritis and swelling

Shoulder

  • Rotator cuff concerns
  • Instability / dislocations (follow-up routing)
  • Impingement and bursitis (evaluation)
  • Arthritis

Hip

  • Hip/groin pain
  • Mobility limits
  • Arthritis evaluation
  • Tendon irritation (evaluation)

Ankle & Foot

  • Sprains and instability
  • Achilles or tendon pain (evaluation)
  • Plantar heel pain (evaluation)
  • Fracture follow-up routing

Hand & Wrist

  • Sprains, strains, and overuse pain
  • Carpal tunnel-type symptoms (evaluation)
  • Trigger finger-type symptoms (evaluation)
  • Fracture follow-up routing

Neck & Back

  • Neck pain and stiffness (evaluation)
  • Low back pain (evaluation)
  • Sciatica-like symptoms (routing)
  • Work/sport-related strain (evaluation)
Urgent symptoms

If you have a visible deformity, cannot bear weight after an injury, severe swelling, new weakness/numbness, fever with a hot painful joint, or new bowel/bladder changes with severe back pain, seek urgent care or go to the emergency room.
