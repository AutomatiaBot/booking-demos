Important

When to Seek Urgent Care

Certain symptoms require immediate medical attention. If you experience any of the following, seek urgent care or go to the emergency room.

Possible Fracture or Dislocation

Visible deformity, bone or joint out of place, or severe pain after trauma. Do not attempt to move or realign the affected area.

Can't Bear Weight or Use Limb

After an injury, complete inability to stand, walk, or use your arm/leg normally may indicate a serious injury requiring immediate evaluation.

Neurologic Red Flags

New or worsening weakness, numbness, tingling, or severe back pain accompanied by bowel or bladder changes.

If symptoms are severe

Go to the nearest emergency room or call 911. If your condition is urgent but stable, call our office at (555) 907-1148 as soon as possible for same-day guidance.

Not Sure If We Can Help?

Call us to discuss your symptoms. Our team can help determine if you need an appointment or if you should seek care elsewhere.
