Patient info

Helpful information for new and returning patients.

Call to schedule

New patient checklist

  • Photo ID + insurance card (if applicable)
  • List of medications and allergies
  • Prior imaging reports (X-ray/MRI) if you have them
  • Brief timeline: when it started, what makes it better/worse

If you want to send records ahead of time, email appointments@peakpointortho.example and call to confirm receipt.

What to expect

  • Review of your history and main concern
  • Focused physical exam
  • Discussion of possible next steps (therapy, imaging, follow-up)
  • Clear guidance on scheduling and timing

We do not provide diagnosis or medication instructions through the website.

When to seek urgent care

If any of the following apply, please seek urgent evaluation.

Possible fracture or dislocation

Visible deformity, bone/joint out of place, or severe pain after trauma.

Can’t bear weight / can’t use limb

After an injury, inability to stand or use the arm/leg normally.

Neurologic red flags

New weakness, worsening numbness, or severe back pain with bowel/bladder changes.

If symptoms are severe

Go to the ER or call local emergency services. If stable but urgent, call (555) 907-1148 as soon as possible.
