What We Offer

Expert Orthopedic Services

Our comprehensive services cover everything from initial evaluation to surgical consultations, ensuring you receive the right care at every stage.

Orthopedic Evaluations

Comprehensive assessment for joint pain, mobility limits, and injury concerns across all major body regions including knee, shoulder, hip, spine, and extremities.

Sports Medicine

Specialized care for acute injuries and overuse conditions in athletes and active adults. We understand the demands of sports and work to optimize your recovery timeline.

Rehab Coordination

Physical therapy referrals, return-to-activity planning, and follow-up coordination to ensure your recovery stays on track with trusted therapy partners.

Injection Consults

General information and scheduling for clinic-offered injections based on your evaluation, including corticosteroid, hyaluronic acid, and other therapeutic options.

Imaging Coordination

Expert guidance for X-ray and MRI studies, plus help coordinating how to send prior imaging for review before your appointment.

Surgical Consults

When indicated, we coordinate consultation pathways for arthroscopy, joint replacement, and other orthopedic surgical procedures.

Prepare for Your Visit

What to Bring & Expect

Being prepared helps us make the most of your appointment time and ensures accurate diagnosis.

What to Bring

  • Photo ID and insurance card (if applicable)
  • Medication list including dosages and any allergies
  • Prior imaging reports or discs (X-ray, MRI, CT)
  • Referral paperwork if required by your insurance
  • Brief timeline of your symptoms and what you've tried

Want to send records ahead of time? Email appointments@peakpointortho.example and call to confirm receipt.
